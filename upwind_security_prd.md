# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## 1. Executive Summary
Introduce **365 FanZone: Live Social Hub & Predictions**, a new interactive feature suite within 365Scores designed to transform passive score checking into an active, social community experience. By enabling synchronized chat rooms, live polls, and micro-predictions, 365Scores will increase daily active user (DAU) session duration and boost retention.

## 2. Personas & Use Cases
- **Fanatic Frank**: Wants to share his excitement or frustration with other fans in real-time during a major derby. He joins the Match FanZone Chat and interacts with poll questions.
- **Bettor Brandon**: Wants to gauge crowd sentiment before placing a live bet. He checks the Live Sentiment Meter and prediction stats.

## 3. Product Scope & Functional Requirements
- **Live Match FanZone**: A dedicated tab in the match details view.
- **Live Sentiment Meter**: Real-time poll asking 'Who will win?' or 'Who will score next?' with animated results.
- **Prediction Leaderboard**: A gamified leaderboard showing users with the most accurate predictions, rewarding them with badges.

## 4. Non-Functional Requirements
- **Low Latency**: FanZone chat and polls must sync within 500ms of game events.
- **Accessibility**: High contrast text and aria-labels for match status.
